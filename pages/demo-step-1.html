Step 1: Mission Intake

The agent gathers the goal, constraints, and stakeholders. Demo Mode seeds realistic details so the flow is repeatable on stage.

Incoming signal

Alert: Executive device flagged with elevated risk signal while traveling.

Goal: Contain risk, preserve business continuity, and deliver a user-ready replacement.

Constraints: Minimum disruption, policy-compliant actions, audit trail required.

Agents on the team

Ops Agent: device and fleet actions

Security Agent: policy + containment

Procurement Agent: replacement logistics

Support Agent: user-ready handoff

Lenovo apps/components in Mission Intake

Lenovo xIQ: Orchestrates the mission, assigns specialist agents, and tracks progress.

ThinkShield: Policy guardrails and risk signal evaluation before actions are proposed.

Lenovo Fleet / Device Management: Device identity, compliance posture, last check-in.

Lenovo Qira: Ambient user context + cross-device handoff to keep the user moving.

Lenovo TruScale / Services: Entitlements and replacement eligibility/SLA confirmation.

Lenovo Support Experience: Case creation and auditable handoff to humans.
